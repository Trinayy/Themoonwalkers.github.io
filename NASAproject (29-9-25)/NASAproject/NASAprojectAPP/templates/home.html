Bio adversity hub

Our core mission is to collect, curate, and disseminate the most current, accurate, and relevant data regarding biological research in extraterrestrial settings. We aim to be the definitive resource for researchers, students, educators, and space enthusiasts interested in:

Lunar Biology

Understanding how life forms, from microbes to complex plant life, behave and adapt in the Moon's reduced gravity and radiation environment.

Who We Are?!

We are a dedicated team of science communicators, space enthusiasts, and former researchers passionate about the intersection of biology and space exploration. We believe that unlocking the secrets of life beyond Earth is key to the future of human space settlement and the advancement of fundamental biological knowledge.
